The Story

Six friends. One prophecy. A world they never knew existed.

When Peter arrives at the Paris Luminatics, he expects cheering crowds and maybe a normal day. Instead he finds shadowy figures, a mysterious green device, hidden tunnels, and a prophecy that seems to know too much.

Peter and his friends are the Ascendants — a team of kids with unusual powers, big problems, and one very unlucky monster magnet named Cody. Together they must follow the clues beneath the Preditorium, face dangerous guardians, survive magical traps, uncover the secrets of Atlantis, and search for the legendary Golden Statue.

The Author

Praanav J Thaakur

Praanav J Thaakur is a 13-year-old Indian author who loves building fantasy worlds, characters, powers, and mysteries. The Preditorium Prophecy: The Golden Statue is his debut novel — a story about courage, friendship, imagination, and the thrill of discovering a world far bigger than expected.

He wrote it for readers who enjoy fantasy quests, secret worlds, monsters, mystery, and friendship adventures — and to show other young people that a big idea, plus the discipline to finish, can become a real book.
