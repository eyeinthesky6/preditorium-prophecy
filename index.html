The Story

Six friends. One prophecy. A world they never knew existed.

The Preditorium Prophecy: The Golden Statue is a middle-grade fantasy adventure for readers aged 8 and up — a secret-world quest for fans of Percy Jackson and Keeper of the Lost Cities.

When Peter arrives at the Paris Luminatics, he expects cheering crowds and maybe a normal day. Instead he finds shadowy figures, a mysterious green device, hidden tunnels, and a prophecy that seems to know too much.

Peter and his friends are the Ascendants — a team of kids with unusual powers, big problems, and one very unlucky monster magnet named Cody. Together they must follow the clues beneath the Preditorium, face dangerous guardians, survive magical traps, uncover the secrets of Atlantis, and search for the legendary Golden Statue.

The Author

Praanav J Thaakur

Praanav J Thaakur is a 13-year-old Indian author who loves building fantasy worlds, characters, powers, and mysteries. The Preditorium Prophecy: The Golden Statue is his debut novel — a story about courage, friendship, imagination, and the thrill of discovering a world far bigger than expected.

He wrote it for readers who enjoy fantasy quests, secret worlds, monsters, mystery, and friendship adventures — and to show other young people that a big idea, plus the discipline to finish, can become a real book.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is The Preditorium Prophecy: The Golden Statue about?

It’s a middle-grade fantasy adventure. Six friends — the Ascendants — discover strange powers and follow a prophecy beneath the Preditorium, through hidden tunnels and magical traps, toward the secrets of Atlantis and the legendary Golden Statue.

What age group is the book for?

It’s written for readers aged 8 and up — a great fit for middle-grade and early-teen fantasy fans (roughly ages 8–14) and anyone who loves a fast, imaginative adventure.

Is it similar to Percy Jackson or Harry Potter?

If you enjoy the secret-world, found-family and mythical-quest feeling of series like Percy Jackson, Harry Potter or Keeper of the Lost Cities, The Preditorium Prophecy hits those notes — kids with unusual powers, a hidden world, and a race against darkness.

Who is the author, Praanav J Thaakur?

Praanav J Thaakur is a 13-year-old Indian author. The Preditorium Prophecy is his debut novel, written to show other young people that a big idea — plus the discipline to finish it — can become a real published book.
